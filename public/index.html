HRealism Meets It's Limits

Take flight with Microsoft Flight Simulator, where the world is at your fingertips. From iconic landmarks to remote destinations, you can explore the globe with stunning accuracy, thanks to real-time satellite data and advanced AI. Whether soaring over towering mountains, vast oceans, or bustling cities, every detail is rendered in breathtaking 4K, making each flight an unforgettable journey.

Choose from a wide range of meticulously crafted aircraft, from nimble light planes to massive commercial airliners, each with authentic cockpits and flight dynamics. Navigate through real-world weather systems, face unpredictable turbulence, and experience live air traffic as you fly through dynamic and ever-changing skies. Whether you're a seasoned pilot or new to aviation, MSFS offers endless opportunities to test your skills and explore.

More than just a flight simulator, Microsoft Flight Simulator is a community. Share your adventures with others, take part in live events, and enjoy an ever-expanding selection of third-party content and mods. With VR compatibility, multiplayer modes, and constant updates, every flight in MSFS promises new thrills and discoveries. Your next great adventure is just a takeoff away!
